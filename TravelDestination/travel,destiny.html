About Maldives

The Maldives, a tropical paradise in the Indian Ocean, is known for its stunning white-sand beaches, crystal-clear waters, and vibrant marine life. With its luxury resorts and overwater bungalows, the Maldives is the perfect destination for relaxation and adventure alike.
